How to Play

Press the Play Button

Playing as a guest is also possible, but scores won't be saved to the leaderboard

Select the difficulty of your choice (easy, medium, hard)

Press "Start Quiz"

Each question has a set of choices, choose the correct answer to add up your points

After answering all questions of your respected difficulty, it will show up your score.

Press "Back" to go back or "Leaderboard" to view leaderboard
